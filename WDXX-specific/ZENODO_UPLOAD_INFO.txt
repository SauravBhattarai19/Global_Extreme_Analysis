DATASET DESCRIPTION FOR ZENODO
================================================================================

Title:
------
Supplementary Data for: Do the Wettest Days Occur Together? A Global Analysis 
on Disentangling Precipitation Intensity from Seasonal Timing

Description:
------------
This dataset contains global precipitation concentration and distribution indices
calculated from ERA5 reanalysis data at 0.25° spatial resolution for 1980-2024.

The package includes three main index families:

1. **WDXXR Indices** (Window Duration for XX% of Rainfall)
   - Novel indices measuring temporal concentration of precipitation
   - Includes WD25R, WD50R, WD75R for multiple percentiles
   - Window timing, efficiency metrics, and regime classifications

2. **ETCCDI Indices** (Expert Team on Climate Change Detection and Indices)
   - Standard precipitation indices: PRCPTOT, SDII, CWD, CDD
   - Extreme indices: R10mm, R20mm, R95p, R95pTOT
   - Follows WMO/CCl/CLIVAR guidelines

3. **Concentration Indices**
   - Gini coefficient (temporal inequality)
   - Lorenz curve metrics
   - Shannon entropy
   - Precipitation concentration factor

All processing code and visualization scripts are included for reproducibility.

Keywords:
---------
precipitation, climate indices, WDXXR, ETCCDI, ERA5, temporal concentration,
climate change, extreme precipitation, Gini coefficient, water resources

Data Format:
------------
- NetCDF (CF-compliant)
- Spatial resolution: 0.25° × 0.25° (721 × 1440 grid)
- Temporal coverage: 1980-2024 (45 years)
- Annual indices for each year

Related Publication:
--------------------
Bhattarai, S., Pradhan, N.R., and Talchabhadel, R. (2025). Do the Wettest Days 
Occur Together? A Global Analysis on Disentangling Precipitation Intensity from 
Seasonal Timing. [In Review]

Data Source:
------------
ERA5 Reanalysis: Hersbach et al. (2020)
DOI: 10.1002/qj.3803
